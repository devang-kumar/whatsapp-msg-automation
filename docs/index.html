How it works
1

Download & Open

Download the .exe and double-click to launch. No installation needed.

2

Add Numbers

Paste phone numbers or upload a CSV file with your contacts.

3

Type Message

Write the message you want to send to all contacts.

4

Scan QR Once

Scan the WhatsApp QR code with your phone. Only needed once.

5

Start Sending

Hit send and watch messages go out automatically with live progress.
