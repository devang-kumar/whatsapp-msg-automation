How it works:
  1. Add phone numbers (with country code) manually or via CSV
  2. Type your message
  3. Click Start Sending — each contact opens in WhatsApp with your message pre-filled
  4. Hit Enter or click Send in WhatsApp to deliver each message
📷 WhatsApp Login (QR Code)

Click "Open WhatsApp" to launch the browser on the server, then refresh the screenshot to scan the QR code with your phone.
